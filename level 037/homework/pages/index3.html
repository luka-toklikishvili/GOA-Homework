Sea Lions - Majestic Marine Mammals

Sea lions are highly social animals and are known for their intelligence and playful behavior. They live in large groups called "rafts" and are excellent swimmers.

Fun Facts:

  • Sea lions can reach speeds of up to 25 mph in the water.
  • They have sharp teeth to catch prey like fish and squid.
  • They often "bark" and communicate with each other through vocalizations.

Habitat & Diet:

Sea lions live along coastlines in North America, South America, and parts of Australia. They primarily eat fish, squid, and other marine creatures.
