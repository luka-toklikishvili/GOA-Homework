Action Films

Action films are known for their fast-paced sequences, intense scenes, and heroic characters.

Mad Max: Fury Road (2015)

Directed by George Miller, this post-apocalyptic action film features breathtaking car chases and explosive combat.

The Dark Knight (2008)

Christopher Nolan's iconic Batman film with Heath Ledger's unforgettable performance as the Joker.

Drama Films

Drama films focus on realistic storytelling, character development, and emotional depth.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A timeless tale of friendship and hope, directed by Frank Darabont, set inside a prison.

Forrest Gump (1994)

Directed by Robert Zemeckis, this film tells the heartwarming story of an extraordinary man living through extraordinary times.

Comedy Films

Comedy films are designed to entertain and make you laugh, often featuring humorous characters and situations.

The Hangover (2009)

Directed by Todd Phillips, a bachelor party in Las Vegas leads to chaos and hilarious consequences.

Superbad (2007)

A coming-of-age comedy about two high school friends, directed by Greg Mottola, filled with awkward and hilarious moments.
