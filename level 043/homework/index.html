Batman - The Dark Knight

Introduction

Batman is a fictional superhero created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger. He appears in American comic books published by DC Comics and is one of the most iconic characters in the superhero genre.

History

Batman first appeared in Detective Comics #27 in 1939. His real name is Bruce Wayne, a billionaire businessman who witnessed his parents' murder as a child, driving him to fight crime in Gotham City as Batman.
