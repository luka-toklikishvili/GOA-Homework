Dinosaurs

Interested in dinosaurs? Here are some interesting facts!

Tyrannosaurus Rex

The Tyrannosaurus Rex was one of the most famous and fearsome dinosaurs that lived around 68-66 million years ago.

Velociraptor

The Velociraptor was a small but very fast and intelligent predator. Its body length reached up to 2 meters.

Triceratops

The Triceratops was a herbivorous dinosaur with a small size but great strength, living during the Cretaceous period.
