About White Owls

White owls, particularly the snowy owl, are known for their beautiful white feathers and nocturnal hunting habits. These birds are often associated with wisdom and mystery, thriving in cold, remote environments. They play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of the ecosystem by controlling rodent populations.
