About Bleach

Bleach is a popular Japanese anime and manga series written and illustrated by Tite Kubo. It follows the adventures of Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who gains the powers of a Soul Reaper and battles dark spirits known as Hollows.

Main Characters

  • Ichigo Kurosaki
  • Rukia Kuchiki
  • Renji Abarai
  • Uryu Ishida
  • Orihime Inoue
