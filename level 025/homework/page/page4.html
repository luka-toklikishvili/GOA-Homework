Overview

Batman, created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger, made his first appearance in *Detective Comics #27* in 1939. Bruce Wayne, a billionaire playboy, becomes Batman after witnessing the murder of his parents in a Gotham City alley. Swearing vengeance and justice, he fights crime using his wealth, intellect, and combat skills, all while dealing with his own internal struggles. Batman is often referred to as "The Dark Knight" for his shadowy presence and determination to fight against crime in a city rife with corruption and villains.

Main Characters

  • Bruce Wayne / Batman - The main character and vigilante hero, known for his detective skills and physical prowess.
  • Alfred Pennyworth - Batman's loyal butler and father figure who helps Bruce with his mission as Batman.
  • Joker - Batman's arch-nemesis, a chaotic and unpredictable criminal mastermind with a dark sense of humor.
  • Commissioner James Gordon - The head of Gotham City's police force, often working with Batman to fight crime.
  • Catwoman (Selina Kyle) - A skilled burglar and anti-heroine with a complex relationship with Batman.

Movies

Batman has been portrayed in numerous films, each offering a unique take on the character. Here are some of the most notable movies:

  • Batman Begins (2005) - Directed by Christopher Nolan, this film explores Bruce Wayne's transformation into Batman and his fight against Ra's al Ghul.
  • The Dark Knight (2008) - The sequel to *Batman Begins*, featuring Heath Ledger's iconic performance as the Joker.
  • The Dark Knight Rises (2012) - The final chapter in Nolan's trilogy, where Batman faces the masked villain Bane.
  • Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (2016) - A darker, older Batman, played by Ben Affleck, faces off against Superman.
  • The Batman (2022) - A reboot starring Robert Pattinson as a young and detective-focused Batman.
