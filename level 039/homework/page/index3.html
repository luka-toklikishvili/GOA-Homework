Introduction

Plants are living organisms that help in purifying the air and growing deep into the soil. They are vital for energy production and sustaining life on Earth.

Types of Plants

  • Trees
  • Grasses
  • Flowers
  • Shrubs
  • Vines

Plant Growth

Plants grow mainly through photosynthesis, using sunlight, water, and nutrients from the soil to create energy. Their growth depends on the environment, including light, water, and soil quality.

Importance of Plants

Plants are essential for life on Earth. They produce oxygen, serve as food for humans and animals, and maintain ecological balance by preventing soil erosion and providing habitats for wildlife.
