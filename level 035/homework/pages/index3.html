BMW - Bavarian Motor Works

About BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke) is a renowned German automobile, motorcycle, and engine manufacturing company. Founded in 1916, it is headquartered in Munich, Germany.

BMW is known for producing luxury vehicles with cutting-edge technology, dynamic performance, and stylish designs. The brand is synonymous with precision engineering and innovative features in both cars and motorcycles.

Popular Models

Some of BMW's most popular models include the BMW 3 Series, 5 Series, 7 Series, and the X5 SUV. The company is also known for its M Series, which includes high-performance versions of its regular models.
