Kodala - The Bird of the Highlands

Kodala (Black Kite)

The Kodala, also known as the Black Kite, is a predatory bird found in the mountainous regions of Georgia. It is known for its strong build and dark green and black plumage.

The Kodala has excellent eyesight and impressive flying skills, making it one of the most formidable predators. It primarily hunts small mammals and birds.

Physical Characteristics

The Kodala stands out with its striking dark plumage, which helps it camouflage in its natural habitat. Its long wingspan and slender body allow it to glide effortlessly over large distances.
