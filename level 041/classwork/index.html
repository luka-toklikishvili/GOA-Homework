Batman

The Dark Knight

Batman is a symbol of justice, vengeance, and heroism in the fictional Gotham City. He fights to rid the city of crime using his wits, physical prowess, and state-of-the-art gadgets.

Key Batman Facts

  • Real Name: Bruce Wayne
  • First Appearance: Detective Comics #27 (1939)
  • Home Base: The Batcave under Wayne Manor
  • Arch-nemesis: The Joker
  • Mission: To rid Gotham City of crime and corruption
  • Iconic Gear: The Batmobile, Batarangs, and the Bat-Signal

Batman’s Allies

  • Alfred Pennyworth: Batman's loyal butler and father figure
  • Robin: Batman’s sidekick, often portrayed by multiple characters like Dick Grayson, Jason Todd, and Tim Drake
  • Commissioner Gordon: Head of Gotham City Police Department, Batman's main ally in the force
  • Batgirl: Barbara Gordon, daughter of Commissioner Gordon, who fights crime alongside Batman
