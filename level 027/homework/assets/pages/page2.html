Welcome to the world of Jackie Chan

Jackie Chan is a legendary martial artist, actor, and director known for his iconic films and impressive stunts.

Explore his biography, filmography, and achievements in this comprehensive site dedicated to his life and career.

Biography

Jackie Chan was born on April 7, 1954, in Hong Kong. He began his career in the Hong Kong film industry as a stuntman and later became a global action star, known for his unique blend of martial arts and comedy.

With over 150 films to his name, Jackie has become a household name worldwide, known for his daring stunts and charismatic performances.

Filmography

  • Drunken Master (1978) - One of Jackie’s most iconic films, showcasing his martial arts skills and comedic timing.
  • Police Story (1985) - A defining film in Jackie’s career, combining high-octane action and innovative stunts.
  • Rush Hour (1998) - A blockbuster action-comedy film where Jackie stars alongside Chris Tucker.
  • The Karate Kid (2010) - A remake of the classic, with Jackie portraying the wise mentor, Mr. Han.

Achievements

Throughout his career, Jackie Chan has received numerous accolades, including:

  • Academy Honorary Award for his extraordinary achievements in film (2016)
  • Golden Horse Award for Best Actor
  • Multiple Hong Kong Film Awards
