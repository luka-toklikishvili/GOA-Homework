Biography

Merab Dvalishvili is a Georgian professional mixed martial artist competing in the UFC Bantamweight division. Known for his relentless wrestling and cardio, he has earned the nickname "The Machine."

MMA Record

Wins: 16
Losses: 4
Division: Bantamweight (135 lbs)

Legacy

One of the toughest and most dominant wrestlers in the UFC, Dvalishvili continues to rise as a future title contender.
