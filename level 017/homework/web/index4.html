1967 Ford Mustang Shelby GT500
Overview Specifications Legacy

Overview

The 1967 Ford Mustang Shelby GT500 is an American muscle car legend, designed by Carroll Shelby. Featuring a powerful 7.0L V8 engine, it became an icon of speed and style.

Specifications

Engine: 7.0L 428 Police Interceptor V8
Horsepower: 355 HP
Torque: 420 lb-ft
0-60 mph: ~6.5 seconds
Top Speed: 130 mph

Legacy

Famous for its role as "Eleanor" in *Gone in 60 Seconds*, the GT500 remains a highly sought-after collector's car, with prices soaring above $200,000.
